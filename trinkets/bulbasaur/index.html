Bulbasaur Sprites

These Bulbasaur Sprites interact when you hover over them and are supported in all major browsers (but loads fastest in Google Chrome).

They are not images and instead use a box-shadow, or roughly, 800 box-shadows. I had originally started building it myself, but after almost 300 pixels in, the progress was taking too long, and I switched to Pixelator.

Afterwards, I shortened the colors to 3-character hex values and converted all of my positions to use em, so that I could have some fun with them.

I hope you enjoyed this approach at animating box-shadow pixel art.
